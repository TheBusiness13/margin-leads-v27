A simpler way to run outreach

1. Start with a campaign

A campaign keeps one audience, market, or offer together. Use a template so the recommended sending rules are prepared for you.

Example
UK Amazon Brands · Apollo leads · Amazon Brand Outreach template

2. Import qualified leads

Upload Excel or CSV, or paste rows. Margin Leads will organize prospects and help you prioritize stronger opportunities.

Best practice
Include company, contact name, role, email, category, product, website, and any visible revenue signal.

3. Review the sequence

Each campaign uses four outreach steps. Review the strongest leads and personalize important opportunities before sending.

Controlled follow-up
Email 1 → 4 days → Email 2 → 5 days → Email 3 → 7 days → Email 4

4. Prepare, review, send

Margin Leads never needs to feel technical. Choose a step, review the selected leads, prepare the batch, and send. Advanced tools remain available when needed.
